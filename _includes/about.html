About Our IT Consulting Services

We specialize in providing top-tier IT consulting services, connecting businesses with highly skilled technology professionals for innovative solutions in software development, system architecture, and cloud services.

  • 2019: Founding and Vision

    In 2019, our journey began with a clear vision: to revolutionize the IT consultancy landscape. Our founders, seasoned experts in Agile development, Quality Assurance, and DevOps processes, identified a gap in the market for a consultancy that not only advised but also actively partnered with clients to implement cutting-edge solutions. We started as a small team in a modest office, but with a grand vision to empower businesses with the latest in IT consulting.

  • 2020: Expansion into Sales and Lead Generation

    By 2020, we had established ourselves as leaders in Agile and DevOps consulting. Recognizing the evolving needs of our clients, we expanded our expertise into Sales and Lead Generation processes. This strategic move not only broadened our service portfolio but also allowed us to offer a more holistic approach to IT consultancy. Our unique methodology in integrating sales processes with technical workflows quickly set us apart in the industry, leading to a significant increase in both client base and reputation.

  • 2021: Project Management Solutions

    In 2021, we marked a significant milestone by successfully consulting and implementing processes for companies engaged in HIPAA-compliant and FDA-approved projects. Our tailored approach in Project Management for these highly regulated sectors was met with acclaim, as we navigated the complex compliance requirements with precision and expertise. This achievement not only showcased our capability to handle projects with stringent regulatory demands but also solidified our reputation as a versatile and reliable IT consultancy firm. Our success in this arena attracted partnerships with leading healthcare and pharmaceutical companies, further diversifying our portfolio and expertise.
